# Semiconductor Collapse — Phase 2: Domestic Production Build-Out

**Scenario:** Semiconductor Supply Collapse
**Phase:** 2 of 3
**Timeline:** 18 months – 7 years from declaration
**Trigger:** Phase 1 transition criteria met (see phase-1-triage-and-allocation.md)
**Last Updated:** 2026-05

---

## Phase 2 Objective

Where Phase 1 manages allocation of constrained supply, Phase 2 structurally rebuilds
domestic and allied production capacity to reduce the foundational vulnerability:
near-total dependence on Taiwan for advanced node chip manufacturing.

This phase spans the 5–10 year timeline required to bring advanced node capacity
to meaningful domestic scale. DED's role is to compress this timeline through
anchor-buyer contracts, co-investment, and allied coordination — not to operate fabs.
