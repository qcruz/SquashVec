# AI Unemployment Scenario — Phase 3: Long-Run Normalization

**Scenario:** AI-Driven Structural Unemployment
**Phase:** 3 of 3
**Timeline:** 5–15 years from Phase 1 trigger
**Trigger:** Phase 2 transition criteria met (see phase-2-transition.md)
**Last Updated:** 2026-05

---

## Phase 3 Objective

Phase 3 represents the new normal: a labor market that has structurally absorbed the AI
transition. DED emergency instruments wind down; structural programs transition from
crisis management to permanent infrastructure. The question shifts from managing the
transition to ensuring the long-run economy distributes AI productivity gains broadly
enough to maintain consumer demand, tax revenue, and defense industrial capacity.
