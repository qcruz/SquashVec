## Renewable Growth Rate

| Technology | 2020 Capacity (GW) | 2025 Capacity (GW) | 5-yr Growth |
|---|---|---|---|
| Solar (all) | 76 | 180 | +137% |
| Wind (all) | 122 | 150 | +23% |
| Battery storage | 2 | 28 | +1300% |

Solar growth is accelerating rapidly. Battery storage is the fastest-growing component.
