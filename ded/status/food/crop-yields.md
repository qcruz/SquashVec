## Vulnerability Flags

- **Corn stock-to-use ratio** has been declining — less buffer for a bad year
- **Soybean exports** heavily dependent on China purchasing decisions
- **Wheat** acreage is at multi-decade lows — structural reduction in planted area
- **California specialty crops** highly water-dependent; drought risk elevated
