## Category Color Reference

| Category | Color | Strategic Role |
|----------|-------|----------------|
| Governance | Blue | Unlocks high-level cards; removes instability efficiently |
| Economy | Gold | Fast point growth; generates instability; consumes Environment |
| Culture | Purple | Slow build; strong late game; hard to stabilize |
| Military | Red | Enables steals; supports Governance; increases Culture instability |
| Technology | Teal | Mid-game strength; boosts Military; adds Culture instability |
| Environment | Green | Resource base; consumed by Economy/Technology; valuable late game |
