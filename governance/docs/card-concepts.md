*This is a naming/concept reference, not a mechanics spec. Move individual cards to `planned-cards.md` when ready to design.*
*Cards marked ✓ already exist in cards.js. Cards marked → are already in planned-cards.md.*

---

## How to Read This Document

- **Tier 1** — Essential. Major civilizational concepts; the game feels incomplete without them.
- **Tier 2** — Important. Significant historical/political phenomena with strong design potential.
- **Tier 3** — Niche. Thematic flavor, more specific use cases, or good only once Tier 1–2 is covered.
- Identity concepts = what your civilization IS (system-level philosophy)
- Event/stacking concepts = what your civilization DOES (actions, trends, moments)

## Priority Order for Implementation

Before adding niche cards, the following Tier 1 concepts have the highest impact on game completeness:

**Governance identities missing:** Communism, Socialism, Fascism, Federalism, Parliament, Empire, Totalitarianism, Anarchy

**Economy identities missing:** Capitalism / Market Economy, Welfare State, Mercantilism

**Culture identities missing:** Nationalism, Secularism, Individualism, Collectivism, Fundamentalism

**Military identities missing:** Standing Army, Conscript Army, Naval Power, Mercenary Force, Guerrilla Warfare

**Technology identities missing:** Empiricism, Rationalism, Industrial Paradigm, Information Age

**High-priority events (cross-category):** Revolution, Purge, Dissent (stacking, not just utility), Scapegoating, Gunboat Diplomacy, New Recruits, Siege, Blockade, Industrial Revolution, Printing Press
